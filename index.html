About

Chess Piece Trainer

A focused tactics trainer that lets you drill puzzles by the piece that delivers the winning move — pawns, knights, bishops, rooks, queens, and kings each have their own pattern language. Pick a piece, solve the first move, and watch the combination play out.

Puzzles are streamed live from a Lichess-derived puzzle database, organized by piece and loaded one at a time to keep the app fast and light — nothing is ever preloaded in bulk.

Built with vanilla HTML, CSS and JavaScript, plus chess.js and chessboard.js. No frameworks, no build step — runs entirely on GitHub Pages.

Keyboard shortcuts: ← / → step through moves, H hint, N next puzzle.
